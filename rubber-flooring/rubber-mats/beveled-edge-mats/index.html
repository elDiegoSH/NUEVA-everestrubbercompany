EVEREST® Beveled Edge Safety Mats

The EVEREST® Beveled Edge Safety Mat is designed with sloped edges that provide smooth and safe transitions between different surfaces. This unique feature eliminates abrupt edges that can cause trips, offering both safety and professional aesthetic finish.

Ideal for high pedestrian traffic areas where safety and accessibility are priorities. Its special design allows perfect integration with existing flooring, creating a continuous, obstacle-free surface.

Safety and Integrated Design

The EVEREST® Beveled Edge Safety Mat offers unique advantages:

  • Sloped edges that prevent trips and falls
  • Smooth transition between different floor levels
  • Easy access for wheelchairs and carts
  • Professional finish and aesthetically pleasing
  • Reduced wear on edges
  • Simple installation without need for metal profiles
  • Enhanced durability in high-traffic areas

Perfect for commercial entrances, offices, public institutions, and any space where pedestrian safety is fundamental.

Beveled Edge Design Advantages

Universal Accessibility

Sloped edges that facilitate passage of wheelchairs, walkers, and carts without obstacles.

Accident Prevention

Eliminates trip risks in transitions between different surfaces or levels.

Regulatory Compliance

Helps comply with accessibility and safety regulations in public spaces.

Enhanced Aesthetics

Professional finish that visually integrates the mat with surrounding flooring.

Traffic Resistance

Design that better distributes load and reduces wear on edges.

Continuous surface that facilitates cleaning with automatic or manual equipment.

Installation and Maintenance

Simple process for professional results

Installation Process

1
Surface Preparation

Clean and level base surface. Ensure it's dry and dust-free.

2
Adhesive Application

Apply recommended rubber adhesive uniformly over the surface.

3
Mat Placement

Position mat carefully, aligning beveled edge according to pedestrian flow.

4
Pressure and Adjustment

Apply uniform pressure to ensure complete adhesion, especially on edges.

5
Curing Time

Respect recommended curing time before allowing pedestrian traffic.

Recommended Maintenance

Daily cleaning with vacuum
Wet cleaning with neutral detergent
Periodic edge inspection
Avoid abrasive chemical products
Protection against sharp objects
Rotation in high-wear areas

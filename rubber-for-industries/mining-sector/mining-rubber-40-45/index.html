40-45 Mining Rubber

40-45 Mining Rubber EVEREST® is a rubber compound with Shore A hardness 40-45, designed to offer the perfect balance between resistance and flexibility. This medium-high hardness makes it ideal for a wide range of mining applications requiring adaptability and durability.

With balanced mechanical properties, 40-45 Rubber provides excellent wear resistance along with good impact absorption capability, making it the preferred solution for versatile applications across various stages of the mining process.

Technical Characteristics

40-45 Mining Rubber EVEREST® offers optimal property balance:

  • Shore A hardness 40-45 for versatile applications
  • Excellent moderate abrasion resistance
  • Good impact absorption capability
  • Maintained flexibility under service conditions
  • Fatigue resistance from repeated flexing
  • Balanced performance across various applications
  • Neutral gray color for standard identification

Ideal for moderate chute linings, transport components, gaskets, and general applications requiring a balance between hardness and flexibility.

Food Grade White Rubber

Food grade white rubber gaskets and seals are specialized materials designed for applications requiring direct contact with food, pharmaceutical products, and substances demanding the highest standards of hygiene and safety.

At Everest Group we offer sanitary white rubber that complies with international regulations, free from contaminants, and resistant to fats, oils, and food processing temperatures.

Industrial Hawaiian Strip Curtains

Hawaiian PVC Strip Curtains are a practical and efficient solution for access control, area separation, and ventilation in industrial, commercial, and warehouse environments.

Manufactured from high-strength, transparent plastic material, they allow the seamless passage of personnel and equipment while maintaining environmental separation, temperature control, and protection against insects and contaminants.

Benefits & Industrial Applications

Our heavy-duty Hawaiian strip curtains offer exceptional advantages for facility management:

  • Effective Access Control - Safe passage for personnel and material handling equipment
  • Superior Ventilation & Airflow - Maintains constant air circulation between zones
  • Optimal Visibility & Transparency - Clear view through PVC strips enhances safety
  • High Impact Resistance - Durable performance in demanding industrial settings
  • Easy Installation - Quick and simple mounting on existing door frames
  • Low Maintenance - Easy to clean and resistant to wear and tear
  • Insect & Pest Barrier - Effective protection for food-grade and sensitive areas

Ideal for warehouse doors, manufacturing areas, cold storage rooms, loading docks, and zone separation in industrial facilities.

Installation & Maintenance Guidelines

Recommendations for optimal performance and longevity

1
Frame Preparation

Ensure the supporting frame or structure is level and in good condition. Measure the installation area to determine the required number of strips.

2
Strip Mounting

Install strips with approximately 50% overlap to guarantee complete coverage. Use appropriate fastening systems for your frame type.

3
Adjustment & Verification

Verify all strips hang freely and make proper contact. Adjust length if necessary for correct operation and seal.

4
Regular Maintenance

Clean periodically with water and mild soap. Regular inspection to detect wear and timely replacement of damaged strips.

Children's Rubber Playground Flooring

EVEREST® Children's Rubber Playground Tiles are a modular safety system specifically designed for children's play areas. Manufactured with high-quality recycled rubber that guarantees maximum safety, durability, and protection for children.

Our 50x50 cm rubber tiles with 2.5 cm thickness offer an ideal solution for playgrounds, daycare centers, schools, and residential areas, providing a safe surface that cushions impacts and prevents injuries.

Superior Safety and Protection

EVEREST® Children's Rubber Playground Tiles stand out for their excellent performance in play areas:

  • Exceptional cushioning that protects children from falls up to 2 meters
  • Anti-slip surface even when wet
  • Eco-friendly material made from non-toxic recycled rubber
  • Weather resistant for indoor and outdoor use
  • Easy installation and maintenance with modular system

This versatility makes them essential in daycare centers, public parks, schools, residential areas, and any space where child safety is a priority.

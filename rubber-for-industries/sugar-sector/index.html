Specialized Rubber for Sugar Industry

In the sugar industry, materials face extreme conditions including abrasion, cane juice exposure, and corrosive processes. EVEREST® rubber products are specifically engineered for sugar mill applications.

Abrasion Resistance

Withstands extreme wear in milling and conveying processes.

Chemical Resistance

Protection against cane juice, molasses, and processing chemicals.

Sugar Mill & Refinery Applications

Targeted solutions for each stage of sugar production

Mill Liners

Sugar mill rubber liners for cane mills that maximize extraction and reduce wear.

  • High abrasion resistance
  • Improved juice extraction
  • Reduced maintenance

Conveyor Belts

Rubber conveyor belts for bagasse, cane, and intermediate product transport.

  • Abrasion resistance
  • Impermeability
  • Easy cleaning

Filter Gaskets

Gaskets and seals for rotary vacuum filters and filtration equipment.

  • Cane juice resistance
  • Complete sealing
  • Long service life

Pump Linings

Rubber linings for juice, molasses, and intermediate product pumps.

  • Corrosion resistance
  • Abrasion protection
  • Cavitation reduction

Safety Flooring

Rubber flooring for process areas with spill risks and wet surfaces.

  • Anti-slip
  • Chemical resistance
  • Easy cleaning

Equipment Protection

Rubber coatings and protectors for equipment against impact and corrosion.

  • Anti-corrosive protection
  • Impact absorption
  • Easy installation

Benefits for Sugar Industry

Increased Efficiency

Improved juice extraction and reduced maintenance downtime.

Cost Reduction

Lower replacement frequency and reduced premature wear costs.

Operational Safety

Anti-slip flooring that prevents accidents in wet areas.

Sanitary Compliance

Materials approved for food product contact.

High-Safety Dielectric Mats

The EVEREST® Dielectric Mat is a high-quality rubber flooring specifically designed to protect personnel from electrical shocks in high-voltage equipment and switchgear areas. Its specialized construction offers maximum safety in critical electrical environments.

In addition to its primary dielectric function, this mat is anti-slip, prevents slips, and provides maximum safety. It features maximum weather resistance, great stability and durability, with excellent impact resistance and built-in anti-fungal protection.

Electrical Protection and Comprehensive Safety

The EVEREST® Dielectric Mat stands out for its advanced technical properties:

  • 30,000 volts dielectric strength for maximum electrical protection
  • High-quality dielectric rubber construction specialized for electrical applications
  • 500 PSI tensile strength for exceptional durability
  • Multi-stripe or ribbed finish anti-slip for safety
  • 1/4" (6.3 mm) thickness for optimal protection
  • 385% elongation for adaptability and resistance
  • Wide temperature range from -20°C to 65°C

Ideal for any area requiring personnel protection from electrical shocks, including control rooms, substations, and electrical equipment areas.

Installation and Maintenance

Recommendations for maximum performance and durability

Installation Recommendations

Clean, dry, and level surface
Use recommended rubber adhesive
Verify electrical continuity of the area
Apply uniform pressure after installation
Perform periodic dielectric tests
Keep free of sharp objects

Preventive Maintenance

Regular Cleaning

Daily sweeping or vacuuming. Wet cleaning with cloth or mop using neutral detergent.

Periodic Inspection

Regularly check for cuts, excessive wear, or damage compromising dielectric protection.

Timely Replacement

Replace immediately if deterioration affecting dielectric properties is detected.

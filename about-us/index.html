Our History

Over 60 years manufacturing high-quality industrial rubber in Mexico

Discover Our Journey

Everest: Mexican Pride, Global Strength

Since 1964, Everest has been synonymous with quality, innovation, and commitment. We were born in Mexico with a clear mission: to manufacture technically specified materials of the highest quality to meet industry needs. From that day, we began writing a story of growth, leadership, and transformation that today transcends borders.

In 1968 we dared to take a decisive step: we ventured into the field of industrial gasketry in rolls and sheets, delivering to the market reliable, resistant materials recognized for their excellence. That was the beginning of a path that has led us to consolidate ourselves as a reference in the industry, always maintaining rigorous professional ethics and delivering what we promise, in every shipment and every project.

Today: Everest at the Summit

Today, Everest is not just a rubber products manufacturer. It's a clean company that acts with responsibility towards the environment. It's a socially responsible company that understands that its success is measured not only in quality and sales, but also in the positive impact it generates in its community and its people.

We are in the process of certifying under the AITF standard, consolidating our commitment to the highest international standards of quality and management. Our technical team is the most complete in the market, composed of specialists capable of solving the most complex challenges and designing solutions tailored to any industry.

Today our footprint reaches Central America, South America, North America, and Europe. We proudly carry the Made in Mexico seal, showing the world that Mexican engineering is synonymous with reliability, innovation, and greatness.

Everest: A Company with Rockstar Soul

What distinguishes us is not just experience or technology. It's attitude.

Every project is like a stage for us; every delivery, like a live concert where we give our best to shine before the audience that matters most: our clients.

At Everest we know that industry needs more than suppliers: it needs reliable allies, robust technical solutions, and a strategic partner that combines tradition, innovation, and social commitment. That's who we are.

That's why Everest is not just a company: It's a symbol of quality, an ambassador of Mexican engineering, and a star in the rubber industry that will continue to set the pace, leading markets, and conquering new frontiers.

Our Responsibility with the Environment

At Everest Group we understand the importance of operating responsibly with the environment. We implement sustainable practices in all our processes:

  • Optimization in the use of natural resources
  • Proper management of industrial waste
  • Reforestation and conservation programs
  • Use of clean energy when possible
  • Development of more eco-friendly products

Our Clean Company distinction reflects our ongoing commitment to environmental protection and sustainability.

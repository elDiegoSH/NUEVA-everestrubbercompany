Available Rubber Types

We offer different compounds according to your specific needs

Natural Rubber

Excellent abrasion resistance and mechanical properties. Ideal for general applications.

  • High elasticity and tear resistance
  • Good mechanical properties
  • Abrasion resistant
  • Temperature: -50°C to 70°C

Neoprene

Resistant to oils, greases, ozone, and weather conditions. Versatile and durable.

  • Resistant to oils and greases
  • Good weathering behavior
  • Ozone and oxidation resistant
  • Temperature: -40°C to 100°C

Nitrile (NBR)

Excellent resistance to oils, fuels, and hydraulic fluids. Widely used in the automotive industry.

  • Excellent oil resistance
  • Good mechanical properties
  • Fuel resistant
  • Temperature: -40°C to 120°C

EPDM

Excellent weather, ozone, and high temperature resistance. Ideal for outdoor applications.

  • Excellent weather resistance
  • Ozone and oxidation resistant
  • Good high-temperature behavior
  • Temperature: -50°C to 150°C

Silicone

Excellent resistance to extreme temperatures. Ideal for food and medical applications.

  • Excellent thermal resistance
  • Food grade approved
  • Good insulating properties
  • Temperature: -60°C to 230°C

Viton (FKM)

Maximum resistance to chemicals and extreme temperatures. For the most demanding applications.

  • Excellent chemical resistance
  • High thermal resistance
  • Resistant to fuels and oils
  • Temperature: -20°C to 200°C

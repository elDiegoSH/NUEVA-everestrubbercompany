Application Instructions

Instructions for correct application

1
Surface Preparation

Clean surfaces of dust, grease, and residues. Some substrates may require cleaning and/or sanding for better adhesion.

2
Adhesive Application

Apply a generous and uniform coat to both surfaces to be bonded. On porous substrates, apply a second coat after the first has dried.

3
Drying Time

Allow solvents to evaporate completely (10-20 minutes depending on environmental conditions) before bonding.

4
Bonding and Pressing

Join the two surfaces and apply uniform pressure of 3-4 kg/cm² for 15 to 30 seconds.

Safety Considerations

Important Precautions

  • Flammable product - keep away from ignition sources
  • Do not expose to high temperatures, sparks, or flames
  • Use with adequate ventilation
  • Avoid contact with skin and eyes
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Store in a cool, dark place

Protective Equipment

  • Chemical resistant gloves
  • Safety glasses
  • Dust and vapor mask
  • Adequate work clothing
  • Mechanical ventilation if necessary

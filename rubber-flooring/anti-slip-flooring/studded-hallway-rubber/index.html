Studded Runner - Anti-Slip Safety Flooring

The EVEREST® Studded Runner is a high-quality anti-slip rubber-caoutchouc flooring specifically engineered for inclined, slippery, or high pedestrian traffic areas. Manufactured from 100% virgin rubber, it guarantees exceptional durability and safety.

Its studded finish provides maximum traction in adverse conditions, making it ideal for commercial, industrial, and institutional applications where slip-and-fall accident prevention is a priority.

Safety and Durability for Critical Areas

The EVEREST® Studded Runner stands out for its superior technical properties:

  • Studded finish for maximum anti-slip traction
  • 100% virgin rubber of high quality for exceptional durability
  • Maximum weather resistance for interior and exterior use
  • Resistant to abrasion and wear in extreme conditions
  • UV protection for prolonged outdoor use
  • High impact resistance for high-traffic areas
  • Exceptional flexibility - will not warp or buckle

Ideal for corridors, ramps, inclined areas, and any space requiring high-resistance anti-slip safety flooring.

Recycled Rubber Gym Floor Tiles

EVEREST® Rubber Gym Tiles are a modular system manufactured from high-density recycled rubber, specifically designed for sports facilities and gyms. They offer an ecological, high-performance solution for training surfaces.

Our rubber tiles provide excellent cushioning, durability, and safety, making them ideal for free weight areas, crossfit boxes, and functional training spaces where maximum impact absorption is required.

Superior Resistance & Cushioning

EVEREST® Rubber Gym Tiles stand out for their exceptional performance in sports applications:

  • Exceptional cushioning for joint protection during workouts
  • High-density rubber for impact and wear resistance
  • Anti-slip surface ensuring safety in all exercises
  • Eco-friendly material made from recycled rubber
  • Weather resistant for indoor and outdoor use

This versatility makes them essential in gyms, school sports areas, playgrounds, and outdoor training spaces.

Advantages of Rubber Gym Tiles

Joint Protection

Superior 75% cushioning significantly reduces impact on joints during intense workouts.

Eco-Friendly Material

Manufactured from recycled rubber, contributing to environmental sustainability and circular economy.

Weather Resistant

Ideal for indoor and outdoor use, resistant to adverse weather conditions and UV radiation.

Superior Anti-Slip

Friction coefficient >0.8 guarantees maximum safety even in sweaty conditions.

High Density

Density of 1100-1300 kg/m³ provides exceptional durability and wear resistance.

Waterproof

Resistant to water and moisture, ideal for areas where liquids are spilled during training.

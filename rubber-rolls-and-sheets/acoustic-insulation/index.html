Acoustic Insulation

Acoustic Insulation is a high-density polymer material specifically designed for noise control and vibration absorption applications.

With 45dB sound attenuation and excellent impact and vibration absorption properties, this material is ideal for environments where acoustic control is critical.

Insulation Properties

Acoustic Insulation is characterized by its unique noise control properties:

  • 45dB sound attenuation for effective acoustic control
  • Excellent vibration absorption for structural protection
  • Excellent impact absorption for damping applications
  • High-density polymer composition for maximum durability
  • Good resistance to chemicals, acids and alkalis
  • Temperature range from -25°C to 90°C

These properties make it ideal for industrial, construction, and automotive applications where noise and vibration control is essential.

Unvulcanized EPDM Sponge Rubber Compound - Densities from 0.6 to 2.0

At Everest Group we develop unvulcanized EPDM sponge rubber compounds of the highest quality, designed for industrial applications requiring flexibility, resistance, cushioning, and versatility in processing.

With densities ranging from 0.6 to 2.0, we offer customized solutions that maintain all the exceptional properties of EPDM - resistance to weathering, ozone, high temperatures and chemicals - while providing the ease of processing and unique cushioning characteristics your project requires.

Custom Unvulcanized Rubber Compounds

At Everest Group we formulate completely custom unvulcanized rubber compounds, ideal for molding, extrusion, and specialized manufacturing processes. Our compounds offer great adaptability during processing, allowing to obtain parts with excellent sealing, cushioning, and mechanical performance properties.

We adjust each formulation according to your needs: densities, hardness, thermal behavior, weather resistance, compressibility, as well as compatibility with chemical or industrial environments. Backed by over 60 years of experience, we guarantee quality, consistency, and performance in every batch.

O-Rings, Seals and Gaskets

O-Rings, Seals and Gaskets are critical components in sealing systems that prevent fluid leaks and contamination in industrial, automotive, hydraulic, and other applications.

We offer a wide range of sealing solutions in various elastomeric materials to meet the most demanding requirements of pressure, temperature, and chemical compatibility.

Technical Characteristics

O-Rings, Seals and Gaskets offer unique advantages for sealing applications:

  • Effective sealing against liquids, gases, and contaminants
  • Wide temperature range from -60°C to 300°C
  • High pressure resistance up to 10,000 psi
  • Chemical compatibility with various fluids and media
  • Long service life and excellent durability
  • Easy installation and maintenance
  • Optimized costs based on application and volume

These components are essential to ensure leak-tightness and reliable operation of equipment and systems in various industrial applications.

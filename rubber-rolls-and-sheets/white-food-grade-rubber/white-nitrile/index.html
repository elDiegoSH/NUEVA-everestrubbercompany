White Sanitary Nitrile Rubber

White Sanitary Nitrile Rubber is a food grade material specifically formulated for applications requiring direct contact with food, pharmaceutical products, and substances demanding the highest standards of hygiene and safety.

This white nitrile compound is formulated with FDA-compliant ingredients, offering excellent resistance to oils, fats, and moderate temperatures while maintaining its physical and chemical properties under demanding service conditions.

Sanitary Nitrile Advantages

White Sanitary Nitrile Rubber offers specific benefits for food-related applications:

  • Excellent resistance to food oils and fats
  • FDA certification for direct food contact
  • Wide thermal range from -20°C to 100°C
  • Easy cleaning and disinfection in sanitary processes
  • No transfer of flavors or odors

Ideal for applications in food, pharmaceutical, and beverage industries where maximum hygiene and fat resistance are required.

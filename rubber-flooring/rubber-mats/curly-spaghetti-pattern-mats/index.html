Ribbed/Spaghetti Pattern Mat

The EVEREST® Ribbed/Spaghetti Pattern Mat is designed to enhance your business image and appearance, combining elegance with maximum functionality. Its unique ribbed/spaghetti design offers a premium look while maintaining excellent cleaning properties.

With 15 different colors available, this mat allows complete customization of your establishment's entrance, creating a memorable first impression for your customers and visitors.

Featured Characteristics

The EVEREST® Ribbed/Spaghetti Pattern Mat offers exceptional advantages:

  • Enhances business image and appearance
  • 100% washable for easy maintenance
  • PVC edges or beveled edges for professional finish
  • 15 different colors for complete customization
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Withstand moisture without deterioration
  • Resistant to high temperatures for durability

Ideal for commercial establishment entrances, corporate offices, hotels, and any space where first impression is important.

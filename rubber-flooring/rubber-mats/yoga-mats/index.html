Professional Yoga Mat

The EVEREST® Yoga Mat is designed for practitioners seeking perfect balance between comfort, stability, and durability. Our mat provides ideal traction to maintain poses safely while offering necessary cushioning to protect joints during intense practices.

Whether you're beginning your yoga journey or are an advanced practitioner, our mat adapts to your level, providing the perfect foundation for growth and exploration in your practice.

Features for a Perfect Practice

The EVEREST® Yoga Mat is designed with specialized features:

  • Non-slip surface for maximum stability in poses
  • Optimal cushioning for knee and joint protection
  • Eco-friendly material and toxin-free
  • Superior grip design even with sweat
  • Lightweight and portable for practice anywhere
  • Easy cleaning and quick drying
  • Proven durability for regular practice

Ideal for Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Bikram, and all yoga styles.

Care and Maintenance

Keep your mat like new for longer

Regular Cleaning

After each use, clean with damp cloth and mild solution of water with vinegar or neutral soap.

Deep Cleaning

Monthly, wash with warm water and mild soap. Rinse thoroughly and air dry outdoors.

Storage

Store rolled with practice surface facing out. Avoid prolonged direct sun exposure.

What to Avoid

Do not use aggressive chemicals, bleach, or washing machine. Do not dry in dryer.

Break-in Period

Your new mat may have slight new material smell that will disappear in 1-2 weeks of use.

Lifespan

With proper care, your EVEREST® mat will accompany you for 2-3 years of regular practice.
